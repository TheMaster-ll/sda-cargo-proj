Getting Started

New to CargoPort? This guide will walk you through account setup, the dashboard, and placing your first shipment order.

Creating Your Account

Setting up your CargoPort account takes just a few minutes.

1
Visit the sign-up page.
Go to app.cargoport.io/register and click Create Account.
2
Enter your details.
Provide your full name, work email, company name, and a strong password (minimum 8 characters, one uppercase, one number).
3
Verify your email.
Check your inbox for a verification link. Click it to activate your account. The link expires in 24 hours.
4
Complete your profile.
Add your phone number, preferred currency, and default shipping addresses. This information speeds up future orders.
Business accounts: If you need to manage a team, select "Business" during registration. You can invite team members and assign roles after setup.

Your First Shipment Order

Let us walk through creating your first shipment from start to finish.

1
Click "New Order" on the dashboard.
The quick-action button is located at the top of the dashboard, or use the Orders > New Order menu.
2
Enter the pickup location.
Type the warehouse or pickup address. CargoPort will auto-complete and validate the address.
3
Enter the delivery destination.
Provide the full delivery address including any gate codes or dock numbers in the notes field.
4
Describe your cargo.
Enter the weight, dimensions, and commodity type. If your cargo requires special handling (e.g., hazardous materials, refrigeration), check the appropriate boxes.
5
Choose your transport mode.
Based on your cargo weight, CargoPort will suggest the optimal mode. You can override this recommendation. See the Transport Modes section below for guidance.
6
Review the quote.
CargoPort generates an instant price estimate. Review the breakdown of freight, surcharges, and estimated delivery date.
7
Submit your order.
Click Submit Order. You will receive a confirmation email and your tracking number (format: CP-XXXXXXXX) within seconds.
First-order bonus: New accounts receive a 10% discount on their first shipment, applied automatically at checkout.

Understanding Transport Modes

CargoPort automatically recommends a transport mode based on your cargo weight, but understanding each option helps you make the best choice.

✈

Air Freight

< 500 kg

Fastest option for lightweight, time-sensitive cargo. Typical delivery in 1-3 days internationally.

🚚

Road Freight

500 - 5,000 kg

Cost-effective for mid-weight regional shipments. Flexible scheduling with door-to-door delivery.

⚓

Sea Freight

> 5,000 kg

Most economical for heavy or bulk cargo. Transit times of 2-6 weeks depending on route.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up an account?
Most users complete registration in under 5 minutes. Email verification is instant in most cases.
Can I change the transport mode after submitting an order?
Yes, as long as the shipment has not been picked up. Go to Orders, find your order, and click Edit. Changes to transport mode may affect pricing and delivery estimates.
What happens if my cargo exceeds the weight I entered?
Carriers will weigh cargo at pickup. If the actual weight exceeds your declared weight by more than 5%, you will receive an adjusted invoice reflecting the actual weight charges.
Is my cargo insured?
Basic carrier liability is included with every shipment. For full-value coverage, select the Cargo Insurance add-on during order creation. Coverage is available up to $500,000 per shipment.
How do I cancel an order?
Navigate to the order detail page and click Cancel Order. Orders canceled before pickup incur no charges. Orders canceled after pickup may be subject to a partial freight charge.
Can I ship hazardous materials?
Yes, CargoPort handles DG (dangerous goods) shipments. You must declare the UN number, proper shipping name, and packing group during order creation. Additional documentation and surcharges apply.
How do I get help if something goes wrong?
Use the live chat (available 24/7), email support@cargoport.io, or call +1 (800) 555-CARGO during business hours. For urgent shipment issues, mention "URGENT" in live chat for priority routing.
